I create and share open educational resources (OER) to support teaching and learning in physics and computational science. These resources are freely available for anyone to use, modify, and distribute under a Creative Commons license.

OER-Forge

I develop and maintain OER-Forge, a platform for creating and sharing open educational resources. OER-Forge takes content in the form of markdown, jupyter notebooks, and word documents and converts them into a WCAG compliant website with downloadable sets of resources in a variety of user specified formats (i.e., TXT, TEX, MD, DOCX, PDF, IPYNB). You can find the source code for OER-Forge on GitHub. And the documentation is available here.
