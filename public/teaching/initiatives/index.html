I participate in and lead several initiatives focused on improving science education, building community, and advancing evidence-based teaching practices. These initiatives bring together educators, researchers, and students to share resources and collaborate on improving how we teach science. Below are the initiatives I’m involved with:

Open Physics Education Network

The Open Physics Education Network, an initiative to make physics education accessible to all. It provides openly licensed, accessible resources designed for diverse learners and educators.
