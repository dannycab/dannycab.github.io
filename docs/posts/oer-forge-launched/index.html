OER Forge (and open publishing platform) launched

July 2025 · 1 min · 46 words · Danny Caballero

OER Forge is an open educational resource (OER) platform that I developed to facilitate the creation, sharing, and dissemination of high-quality educational materials. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, allowing educators and learners to easily find and use resources that meet their needs.
